The Program

Are you passionate about social media and college sports?
Then Fancred is for you!

About Fancred

Fancred is on a mission to unite the world's sports fans. We have built a social network dedicated entirely to sports and are selecting three passionate students to help lead and build the community at your university. We're searching for the best fanbase across the country and you'll be competing with other universities.

Ambassador Role

  • Build your school's community on Fancred
  • Provide FAQ support for users and weekly updates to Fancred staff
  • Be active in the Fancred app

Requirements

  • Own an iPhone
  • Current full-time student
  • At least a sophomore
  • Active Twitter, Instagram & Facebook accounts
  • Major in Marketing, Communications/PR, or Sports Management
